Ozuna

Ozuna, born Juan Carlos Ozuna Rosado, is a Puerto Rican singer and songwriter known for his reggaeton and Latin trap music. He has released multiple chart-topping albums and collaborated with artists like Daddy Yankee and Selena Gomez. Ozuna is one of the best-selling Latin music artists of all time.
